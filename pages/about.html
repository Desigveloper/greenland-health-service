OUR STORY

From a single clinic to global enterprise health.

Founded in 2009 by Dr. Amara Osei, GreenLand began as a single occupational health clinic serving manufacturing companies in the Greater Accra Region. Our commitment to evidence-based care and transparent reporting quickly earned us a reputation as the partner enterprises could trust.

Today we serve over 120 enterprises across regulated industries worldwide — from mining and logistics to financial services and technology. Our approach has remained the same: clinical rigour, operational pragmatism, and genuine partnership.
